More About Me!

My background through my earlier years of studying within Simon Fraser University has always been a strong focus on arts. I enjoy the intricate practices and meanings that develop within every artwork that is produce by its creator.

However from my personal hobbies and studies, technology and design always peaked my interest and through that I have found a great passion in delving on how the two art forms and practices can concide with eachother. To create meaning, connect with the user or whatever it may be for the viewer or user in a functional manner.

From this new found passion in both forms of artistical expression. I took interest in UI/UX in where I explore solutions to tackle the frustrations and needs a user has with communication design, graphics and visuals to storytell, striving for acessibility.
