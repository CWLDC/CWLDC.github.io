Project Overview

"Black Out" is an interactive narrative created for a 3rd year course, IAT 313: Narrative and New Media. Which explores narratives in various media and new media formats (such as linear, multi-linear and networked media environments)

Storyboarding and Narrative Ideation

For this project, we explored different media formats such as linear and multi-linear story telling to portray our narrative of a horrific murder and crime event. Similar to game's like Stanley's Parable. Our narrative draws influence on interactive storytelling games in a comic/2d format.

Story Flow

My first task, was to help with the story flow and create potential paths the user can take and it's outcome. Alongside creating dialogue (copy) for the story.

Visual Design & Renders

Upon coming up with the story's narrative and media format. We had to come up with our character renders and graphic visuals. We designed the story to play out in a comic book way and thus the visual graphics and render's were design based on that aesthetic in mind.
